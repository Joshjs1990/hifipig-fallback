Boyer Audio 10-Year Anniversary Celebrations

March 12, 2026

BOYER AUDIO 10-YEAR ANNIVERSARY BEGINS AT AUDIO SHOW DELUXE 2026

Boyer Audio will mark the start of its 10-year anniversary celebrations with a major presence at Audio Show Deluxe 2026, taking place on 21st – 22nd of March at Whittlebury Park.

The UK distributor will run demonstrations across two rooms, Monza and Syndicate 9 presenting systems featuring brands from its portfolio, including a number of UK premieres.

WADAX STUDIO PLAYER COLLECTION MAKES UK PREMIERE AT AUDIO SHOW DELUXE 2026

One of the main debuts will be the Wadax Studio Player Collection, a three-box digital source system consisting of the Player, PSU and Clock connected via Akasa DC cables. Brandon Lauer from Wadax will represent the brand during the event and demonstrate the system in Boyer Audio’s flagship Monza room.

ORPHEUS LAB HERITAGE SERIES AMPLIFICATION ON DEMO

Another UK premiere at the show will be the Orpheus Lab Heritage H Two 33BD preamplifier alongside the Heritage H Three M800 monoblock amplifiers. Operations Manager Tiago Novo will attend to support demonstrations of the Swiss manufacturer’s reference-level amplification.

KROMA ATELIER CALLAS, THAÏS AND JOVITA LOUDSPEAKERS

Several loudspeakers from Kroma Atelier will be used across the Boyer Audio systems. The reference Callas speakers will appear in the Monza room in graphite grey Krion, while the Thaïs model will be used in the Syndicate 9 room in an ocean blue Krion finish. The Jovita speakers will also appear elsewhere at the show in the Vale room with Symmetry Systems.

BASSOCONTINUO GOLIA AND ACCORDEON XL4 2.0 EQUIPMENT RACKS

Equipment support will include the UK debut of the Bassocontinuo Ultimate Line Golia rack, positioned in the Monza room system. In the Syndicate 9 room, Boyer will use a custom Accordeon XL4 2.0 rack finished in white leather from the B by Bassocontinuo range.

ENGSTRÖM ARNE INTEGRATED AMPLIFIER

The Engström ARNE integrated amplifier will form part of the system demonstrated in the Syndicate 9 room during the show.

CINNAMON AUDIO GALLE DAC INTRODUCED IN THE UK

Boyer Audio will also introduce the Cinnamon Audio Galle DAC to UK audiences. The digital source component from the Portuguese manufacturer will be included in the show systems as part of the company’s 2026 line-up.

NETWORK ACOUSTICS TEMPUS SWITCHES USED IN BOTH BOYER ROOMS AT AUDIO SHOW DELUXE 2026

Both demonstration rooms will use Network Acoustics Tempus network switches as part of the digital systems.

SHUNYATA RESEARCH CEO CAELIN GABRIEL TO HOST DEMOS AT AUDIO SHOW DELUXE 2026 WITH BOYER AUDIO

Shunyata Research CEO Caelin Gabriel will travel to the UK for the event and run demonstrations and workshops in the Syndicate 9 room alongside Richard Rogers. Boyer notes that Shunyata Research was one of the first brands it partnered with when the distributor launched in 2016.

Products from the company on demonstration will include the Everest-X shelf unit, Omega-X speaker cables and interconnects, Omega-X QR and XC power cables, the Altaira-X grounding system, and the Delta-X power distributor.

CAELIN GABRIEL EVENT AT KJ WEST ONE

Following Audio Show Deluxe 202, Caelin Gabriel will also appear at KJ West One in London for a separate event titled An Evening With Caelin Gabriel on 26th of March.

HiFi PiG Says: A very happy 10th Anniversary to Boyer Audio, we can’t wait to hear their systems at Audio Show Deluxe 2026 next week!
